Our History

Plant Palette began as a dream to create a warm, welcoming space where delicious plant-based food could inspire healthier living and spark community connection. After years in the culinary world, founder Amara was driven to build more than a restaurant - she envisioned a hub for sustainability, transparency, and good food made with care. Today, every dish tells a story - from local farms to vibrant plates - welcoming vegans, vegetarians and curious food lovers alike.

From day one, Plant Palette set out to create more than just a place to eat - we built a space where people could connect, learn, and celebrate the power of plants. Our team is trained in both culinary craft and the values that shape our mission, from sustainable sourcing to community care. Every dish is part of a larger story - of farmers, flavour, and the joy of eating with purpose.
